Overview

The College of Humanities and Development of Agricultural University of China was established in end of 2002. It was founded on the basis of the former College of Humanities and Social Sciences, the College of Rural Development and other departments. The research work, discipline construction and personnel training of the college have been developed rapidly, and have been built into a comprehensive system with the functions of education, research, training and consulting services, with rich academic achievements. Social and policy impacts are significant and international exchanges and cooperation are extensive.
