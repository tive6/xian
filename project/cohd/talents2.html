Postgraduate Education

In end of 2002, the College of Humanities and Development of Agricultural University of China was established on the basis of the former Institute of Rural Development, College of Humanities and Social Sciences and other departments. Graduate education in our college, has made rapid progress and development, has established a relatively complete discipline, the training level is perfect. Advanced research personnel training system with comprehensive and relatively complete education system. There are two Ph.D. programs in the field of rural development and management, sociology (in the process of examination and approval); Education, Economics and Management, Sociology, Economic Law, Rural Development and Management, Regional Economics, Social Security, English language and Literature, Communication, etc. The college has 361 graduate students (257 master students, 104 doctoral students and 17 doctoral supervisors). 52 graduate tutors (for teachers, please refer to the link to r. Colr. Col22316. Index. html.). The research field and direction of graduate student training in our college is in a unique position in the field of rural social science in China. The cultivation of postgraduates has had a wide range of influence, rural development management, rural sociology and other major and direction of training and education work in the leading position in the country. The research work and achievements made by tutors and graduate students have great influence at home and abroad.

1 Rural Regional Development

With the development and management of rural master began to recruit students in 2004, 2008 through the assessment of key disciplines for the Beijing City, is a major subject in the school 985 and 211 Engineering project construction. The existing 18 doctoral tutor, 15 master trainers, has graduated nearly 200 doctoral students. The college aims to cultivate the International Perspective of industry study on the development of advanced management methods, grasp the development, management and practice of personnel, government employment direction for colleges and universities, scientific research institutions, enterprises, institutions and development at home and abroad research and management positions. In the training process pay attention to the combination of theory and practice, to encourage graduate students to independent thinking and innovation. In the field of poverty and anti poverty left behind, population, natural resource management, citizen participation, public policy and development intervention, and international development cooperation to carry out a large number of in-depth research, has formed its own unique perspective, The social policy and extensive and far-reaching impact. The doctoral enrollment for admission examination system selection.
