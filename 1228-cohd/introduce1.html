Overview

The College of Humanities and Development of Agricultural University of China was established in end of 2002. It was founded on the basis of the former College of Humanities and Social Sciences, the College of Rural Development and other departments. The research work, discipline construction and personnel training of the college have been developed rapidly, and have been built into a comprehensive system with the functions of education, research, training and consulting services, with rich academic achievements. Social and policy impacts are significant and international exchanges and cooperation are extensive.
Under the development of management department, legal department, Department of sociology, media department and foreign languages department; the existing staff of 191 people, including 36 professors, 94 associate professors, 41 lecturers, 1 deputy editor, editor of 1 people, 3 senior experimental division, the Division 2 people, 1 senior workers people, staff of 9 people. Among them, 2 people to enjoy the special allowance of the State Council, 2 people selected by the Ministry of education "program for new century excellent talents, 1 for Beijing city 100 new century social science talent (the second group), 8 people in Beijing higher school" Young Talents Program ", 2 people received the" DaBeiNong "the teacher teaching awards, 2 people received DaBeiNong" Young Scholar Award.
The main research areas include the development of school management and intervention in rural areas, China foreign aid, rural social problems and social management in rural areas, the rule of law and politics, poverty and poverty reduction, environmental and resource management, agricultural sociology, cultural anthropology, rural communication and so on.
